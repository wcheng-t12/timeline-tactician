📜 Timeline Tactician

Put history in order! Each round gives you 5 events or inventions in a random order — swap their positions until they're arranged from oldest to newest, then submit before time runs out.

How to Play

  • 🗂️ Each round shows 5 cards representing events, inventions, or milestones — shuffled randomly.
  • 👆 Tap/click one card to select it (it lifts up).
  • 👆 Tap/click a second card to swap their positions.
  • 🎯 Arrange the cards from OLDEST (left) to NEWEST (right).
  • ✅ Press Submit to check your order — correct positions turn green, incorrect ones turn red.
  • ❌ An incorrect submission costs 1 life (❤️) — but you can keep adjusting and resubmit.
  • ⏱️ Each round has a time limit shown by the bar at the top.
  • 🏆 Complete all 4 rounds before running out of lives. Your score determines your star rating!
