🚀 Launch Lab

Apply real projectile motion physics! Adjust the launch angle and power, account for the wind, and fire your projectile to hit each target.
Watch the trajectory preview update live as you tune your shot.

How to Play

  • 🎯 Each round has a target placed at a different distance (and sometimes height).
  • 📐 Use the Angle slider (10°-80°) to set your launch angle.
  • 💪 Use the Power slider to set launch speed.
  • 🌬️ Watch the Wind indicator — it pushes your projectile left or right!
  • 👀 A dotted trajectory preview updates live so you can aim before firing.
  • 🔥 Press Fire! to launch. You get 3 shots per round.
  • ✅ Hit the target for points — faster hits and fewer shots used = more points.
  • ❌ Running out of shots or time without a hit costs a life (❤️).
  • 🏆 Complete 6 rounds. Your final score determines your star rating!
